# Privacy Policy for Teloscopy

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview

Teloscopy ("we", "our", "the app") is a genomic intelligence platform that provides biological age estimation, disease risk prediction, and personalized nutrition recommendations. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect

### Images
- Face photos uploaded for analysis are transmitted to our server for processing
- Images are processed in real-time and are **not stored** after analysis is complete
- We do not use your images for training machine learning models

### Profile Information
- Age, biological sex, and ancestral region (used for analysis calibration)
- Genetic variant identifiers (e.g., rs429358:CT) if voluntarily provided
- Dietary restrictions and preferences

### Technical Data
- Device type and Android version (for compatibility)
- App version
- Crash reports (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used **exclusively** to:
1. Perform the requested genomic analysis
2. Generate disease risk assessments
3. Create personalized nutrition recommendations
4. Improve app stability (crash reports only)

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Share your genetic information with insurance companies, employers, or any other entity
- Store analysis results on our servers after delivery to your device

## Data Storage

- Analysis results are stored **only on your device**
- The server processes requests in memory and does not persist user data
- No user accounts are required to use the app

## Data Transmission

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
- Data is sent only to the configured Teloscopy server
- No data is sent to third-party analytics services

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Use the app without providing genetic data (profile-only mode)
- Delete all locally stored data by clearing the app's data or uninstalling
- Request information about any data we may hold (contact us below)

## Children's Privacy

Teloscopy is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Medical Disclaimer

Teloscopy is for **research and educational purposes only**. It is not a medical device and should not be used for diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of any disease. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
