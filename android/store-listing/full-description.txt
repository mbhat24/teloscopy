Teloscopy is a cutting-edge genomic intelligence platform that combines computer vision, genetic analysis, and nutritional science to provide personalized health insights.

KEY FEATURES

Biological Age Estimation
Upload a face photo and our advanced computer vision pipeline analyzes facial markers — skin texture, wrinkle patterns, symmetry, and pigmentation — to estimate your biological age and telomere length. Understand how your lifestyle affects your cellular aging.

Disease Risk Prediction
Assess your genetic disease risk using a comprehensive database of 519 known SNP variants. Input your genetic data (from services like 23andMe or AncestryDNA) and receive risk assessments for cardiovascular disease, Alzheimer's, diabetes, and more. Each risk comes with contributing factors and actionable recommendations.

Personalized Nutrition
Receive AI-generated diet plans tailored to your genetic profile, ancestral region, and dietary preferences. Our system draws from a database of 551+ foods across 30 world regions to create culturally appropriate, genetically optimized meal plans. Supports vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and many more dietary restrictions.

Profile-Only Analysis
Don't want to upload a photo? Use Profile-Only mode to get disease risk assessments and nutrition recommendations based solely on your age, sex, region, and genetic variants.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Take or upload a face photo
2. Enter your profile details (age, sex, ancestral region)
3. Optionally add known genetic variants
4. Receive comprehensive results: biological age, disease risks, and a personalized diet plan

POWERED BY SCIENCE
- Telomere length estimation based on Muezzinler et al. (2013) research
- Disease risk models calibrated against published GWAS studies
- Nutrition recommendations following WHO and regional dietary guidelines
- Perceived age estimation inspired by Christensen et al. (2009)

PRIVACY FIRST
- No account required
- Images are processed and immediately discarded
- No personal data is stored on our servers
- All analysis is performed in real-time

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
Teloscopy is designed for research and educational purposes only. It is not intended for medical diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of any disease. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice.
