## App Name

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Teloscopy is a cutting-edge genomic health companion that uses computer vision and genetic data to provide personalised health insights directly from your smartphone.

FACIAL ANALYSIS
Take a selfie or upload a photo to receive a biological age estimate based on skin texture, wrinkle patterns, pigmentation uniformity, and other scientifically-grounded facial biomarkers. See how your skin health compares to your chronological age.

DISEASE RISK ASSESSMENT
Enter your known genetic variants (e.g., APOE, BRCA) and Teloscopy will calculate your risk profile for cardiovascular disease, Alzheimer's, various cancers, and more. Each risk is colour-coded with detailed probability scores, contributing factors, and evidence-based recommendations.

PERSONALISED NUTRITION
Based on your genetic profile, age, and ancestry, Teloscopy generates a tailored nutrition plan including:
- Daily calorie targets
- Key nutrients to prioritise
- Foods to increase and avoid
- Multi-day meal plans respecting your dietary restrictions (vegan, halal, gluten-free, and more)

PROFILE-ONLY MODE
Don't want to upload a photo? Use profile-only analysis with just your age, sex, region, and known variants for disease risk and nutrition recommendations.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your photos are never stored -- they are processed in memory and discarded immediately. No tracking, no ads, no data selling. Your health data stays yours.

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
Teloscopy is for informational and educational purposes only. Results are not medical diagnoses. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical decisions.
