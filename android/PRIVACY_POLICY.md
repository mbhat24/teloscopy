# Privacy Policy for Teloscopy

**Last updated: April 1, 2026**

## Introduction

Teloscopy ("we", "our", or "the App") is a genomic health analysis application that provides biological age estimates, disease-risk assessments, and personalised nutrition recommendations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Facial photographs**: Used solely for facial analysis to estimate biological age and skin health metrics. Photos are transmitted to the Teloscopy backend server for processing and are not stored after analysis completes.
- **Profile data**: Age, sex, ancestry/region, known genetic variants, dietary restrictions, and optional telomere length measurements.
- **App preferences**: Theme settings and server configuration stored locally on your device.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or tracking data.
- We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social media trackers.
- We do not access your contacts, calendar, location, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

## How We Use Your Information

All data you provide is used exclusively to:
1. Perform the requested genomic and facial analyses.
2. Generate disease-risk assessments and nutrition recommendations.
3. Display results within the app.

## Data Processing and Storage

- **On-device**: App preferences are stored locally using Android DataStore. No health data is cached on device after you close the results screen.
- **Server-side**: Facial images and profile data are transmitted to the Teloscopy backend server over your network connection. Images are processed in memory and discarded immediately after analysis. Profile data is used only for the duration of the analysis request.
- **No cloud storage**: We do not store your facial images, genetic variants, or health results on any cloud service or database beyond the duration of a single analysis request.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing analysis results directly to you.

## Data Security

- All network communication between the app and backend uses your configured server endpoint.
- For production deployments, we strongly recommend configuring HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.
- Facial images are processed in server memory and never written to disk.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data the app holds about you (displayed directly in the app).
- **Deletion**: All analysis data is ephemeral and not persisted. Close the app to discard all results.
- **Opt-out**: You can use profile-only analysis without providing facial images.

## Children's Privacy

Teloscopy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Health Disclaimer

Teloscopy provides estimates and assessments for informational and educational purposes only. Results are not medical diagnoses and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
